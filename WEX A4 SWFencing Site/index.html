Welcome to the South West Fencing

The South West Region comprises the counties of Bristol, Cornwall, Devon, Dorset, Gloucestershire, Isles of Scilly and Somerset. The fencing facilities in the region comprise the Clubs, a centre of education and training for fencers, coaches and organisers of the sport.
